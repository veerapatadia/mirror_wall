# mirror_wall_app

## Project Description

Mirror Wall is an intuitive and versatile browser app designed to enhance your web-browsing experience. With the flexibility to switch between different search engines on the go, Mirror Wall ensures that you always have the best tools at your fingertips. The app features a seamless bookmarking system, allowing you to easily save and manage your favorite websites with just a tap. Additionally, Mirror Wall includes a robust connectivity check that ensures you're always aware of your internet status, preventing frustrating interruptions and providing a smoother browsing experience. Whether you're searching for information, exploring new sites, or revisiting your favorite pages, Mirror Wall is your reliable companion in the digital world.
